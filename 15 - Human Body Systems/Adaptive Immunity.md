# Adaptive Immunity

## Overview
A vertebrate-specific defense that is mediated by B lymphocytes ([[B Cells]]) and T lymphocytes ([[T Cells]]).

It exhibits specificity, memory, and self-nonself recognition. It is slower to activate than [[Innate Immunity]] but provides long-lasting protection (the basis for vaccines).
