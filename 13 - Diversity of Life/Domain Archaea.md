# Domain Archaea

## Overview
One of the two prokaryotic domains of life.

Consists of [[Prokaryotic Cells]] that often live in Earth's extreme environments (extremophiles like halophiles, thermophiles, and methanogens). They lack peptidoglycan in their cell walls and share some traits with [[Domain Eukarya]].
