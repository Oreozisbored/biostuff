# Arthropods

## Overview
Phylum Arthropoda. The most diverse and numerous animal phylum.

Characterized by a segmented body, hard exoskeleton (chitin), and jointed appendages. Includes insects, arachnids (spiders, ticks), myriapods (centipedes), and crustaceans (crabs, lobsters).
