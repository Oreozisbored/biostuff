# Echinoderms

## Overview
Phylum Echinodermata. Slow-moving or sessile marine animals.

Characterized by a rough or spiny skin, a water vascular system with tube feet, and (usually) pentaradial symmetry in adults. Includes sea stars, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers. Closely related to [[Chordates]].
