# Plants Overview

## Overview
Multicellular eukaryotes that are photosynthetic autotrophs ([[Producers]]).

They evolved from green algae. Key adaptations for land include a waxy cuticle, stomata, and vascular tissue. Groups include [[Bryophytes]], [[Seedless Vascular Plants]], [[Gymnosperms]], and [[Angiosperms]].
