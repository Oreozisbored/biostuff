# Autosomal Recessive Disorders

## Overview
Genetic disorders caused by a recessive allele on an autosome. Two copies are required for expression.

Can "skip" generations in a [[Pedigree]] and often appear in offspring of asymptomatic [[Carrier]] parents. Examples: Cystic fibrosis, Tay-Sachs, Sickle-cell disease.
