# ABO Blood Types

## Overview
A human blood group system based on the presence or absence of carbohydrates A and B on red blood cells.

An example of both [[Multiple Alleles]] (IA, IB, i) and [[Codominance]] (IA and IB are codominant to each other, but dominant over i). Determines blood transfusion compatibility.
