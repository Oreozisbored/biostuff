# Griffith Experiment

## Overview
A 1928 experiment by Frederick Griffith.

He injected mice with two strains of bacteria (harmless R strain, deadly S strain). He found that heat-killed S bacteria could transform living R bacteria into deadly S bacteria. This suggested the existence of a "transforming principle" that carried genetic information. See [[Avery Experiment]].
