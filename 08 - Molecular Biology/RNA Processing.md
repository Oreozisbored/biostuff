# RNA Processing

## Overview
Modification of RNA primary transcripts (pre-mRNA) in eukaryotes, occurring before the RNA leaves the nucleus.

Includes the addition of a 5' cap and a 3' poly-A tail, and RNA splicing (removal of [[Introns and Exons|introns]]). Prepares the [[mRNA]] for [[Translation]].
