# Translation

## Overview
The synthesis of a polypeptide using the genetic information encoded in an [[mRNA]] molecule. 

There is a change of "language" from nucleotides to amino acids. Occurs at [[Ribosomes]] in the cytoplasm, utilizing [[tRNA]] and the [[Genetic Code]]. Divided into initiation, elongation, and termination.
