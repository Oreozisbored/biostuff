# DNA

## Overview
Deoxyribonucleic acid. A double-stranded, helical nucleic acid molecule, consisting of nucleotide monomers with a deoxyribose sugar and the nitrogenous bases adenine (A), cytosine (C), guanine (G), and thymine (T). 

Capable of being replicated and determining the inherited structure of a cell's proteins. See [[DNA Structure]] and [[Central Dogma]].
