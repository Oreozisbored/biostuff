# Hershey-Chase Experiment

## Overview
A 1952 experiment by Hershey and Chase using bacteriophages (viruses that infect bacteria).

They used radioactive sulfur (35S) to tag viral proteins and radioactive phosphorus (32P) to tag viral [[DNA]]. They found that only the 32P-tagged DNA entered the bacterial cells, definitively proving that DNA is the genetic material.
