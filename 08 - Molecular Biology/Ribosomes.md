# Ribosomes

## Overview
Complexes of [[rRNA]] and protein that serve as the site of [[Translation]].

They consist of a large and small subunit. They have three binding sites for tRNA:
- **A site**: Holds the incoming tRNA carrying the next amino acid.
- **P site**: Holds the tRNA carrying the growing polypeptide chain.
- **E site**: The exit site.
