# Calvin Cycle

## Overview
The second of two major stages in [[Photosynthesis]] (following the [[Light-Dependent Reactions]]), involving the fixation of atmospheric CO2 and reduction of the fixed carbon into carbohydrate (G3P).

Occurs in the stroma of the chloroplast. Requires [[ATP]] and NADPH. Guided by the enzyme [[RuBisCO]].
