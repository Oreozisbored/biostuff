# Glycolysis

## Overview
A series of reactions that ultimately splits glucose into two molecules of pyruvate. It occurs in almost all living cells in the cytoplasm.

**Yield**: 2 net [[ATP]], 2 [[NAD+ and NADH|NADH]], 2 pyruvate.
It is an anaerobic process (does not require oxygen).
