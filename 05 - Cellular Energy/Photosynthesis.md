# Photosynthesis

## Overview
The conversion of light energy to chemical energy that is stored in sugars or other organic compounds.

Occurs in [[Chloroplasts]] of plants, algae, and some prokaryotes.
Overall equation: 6CO2 + 6H2O + Light Energy -> C6H12O6 + 6O2.

## Stages
1. [[Light-Dependent Reactions]] (in [[Thylakoids]])
2. [[Calvin Cycle]] (Light-Independent Reactions, in stroma)
