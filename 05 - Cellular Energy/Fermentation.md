# Fermentation

## Overview
A catabolic process that makes a limited amount of [[ATP]] from glucose without an electron transport chain and that produces a characteristic end product.

It consists of [[Glycolysis]] plus reactions that regenerate NAD+ so glycolysis can continue. Two main types: [[Lactic Acid Fermentation]] and [[Alcoholic Fermentation]].
