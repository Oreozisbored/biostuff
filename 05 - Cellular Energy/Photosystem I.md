# Photosystem I

## Overview
A light-capturing unit in a chloroplast's thylakoid membrane.

It absorbs light to re-energize the electrons that traveled down the ETC from [[Photosystem II]]. These high-energy electrons are ultimately used to reduce NADP+ to NADPH, which carries them to the [[Calvin Cycle]].
