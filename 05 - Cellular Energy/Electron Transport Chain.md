# Electron Transport Chain

## Overview
A sequence of electron carrier molecules (membrane proteins) that shuttle electrons during the redox reactions that release energy used to make [[ATP]].

Located in the inner mitochondrial membrane. Electrons are donated by [[NAD+ and NADH|NADH]] and [[FAD and FADH2|FADH2]]. Oxygen acts as the final electron acceptor, forming water.
