# ATP

## Overview
Adenosine Triphosphate (ATP) is the primary energy currency of the cell.

Structure consists of adenine, ribose, and a chain of three phosphate groups. Energy is released when the terminal phosphate bond is broken via [[Hydrolysis]], forming [[ADP]]. This energy is used to drive [[Endergonic Reactions]].

See also: [[Cellular Respiration]], [[Photosynthesis]].
