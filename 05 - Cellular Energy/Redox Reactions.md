# Redox Reactions

## Overview
A chemical reaction involving the complete or partial transfer of one or more electrons from one reactant to another.

- **Oxidation**: Loss of electrons (OIL - Oxidation Is Loss).
- **Reduction**: Gain of electrons (RIG - Reduction Is Gain).

Central to both [[Cellular Respiration]] and [[Photosynthesis]].
