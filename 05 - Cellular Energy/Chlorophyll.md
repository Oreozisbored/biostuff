# Chlorophyll

## Overview
A green pigment located within the [[Chloroplasts]] of plants and algae and in the membranes of certain prokaryotes. 

Chlorophyll a participates directly in the light reactions, which convert solar energy to chemical energy. It absorbs red and blue light and reflects green. See [[Photosynthesis]].
