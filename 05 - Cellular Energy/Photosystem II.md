# Photosystem II

## Overview
One of two light-capturing units in a chloroplast's thylakoid membrane.

It absorbs light, exciting electrons that are passed to an electron transport chain. It replaces its lost electrons by splitting water, which releases oxygen as a byproduct. Functions *before* [[Photosystem I]].
