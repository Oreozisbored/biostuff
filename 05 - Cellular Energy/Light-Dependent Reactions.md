# Light-Dependent Reactions

## Overview
The first of two major stages in [[Photosynthesis]]. 

Occur on the thylakoid membranes of the [[Chloroplasts]]. They convert solar energy to the chemical energy of [[ATP]] and NADPH, releasing oxygen in the process (by splitting water). Involves [[Photosystem II]] and [[Photosystem I]].
