# Pyruvate Oxidation

## Overview
The conversion of pyruvate (from [[Glycolysis]]) into acetyl CoA, with the release of CO2 and the production of [[NAD+ and NADH|NADH]]. 

Occurs in the mitochondrial matrix and serves as the link between glycolysis and the [[Krebs Cycle]].
