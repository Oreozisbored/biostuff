# Cellular Respiration

## Overview
The catabolic pathways of aerobic and anaerobic respiration, which break down organic molecules and use an electron transport chain for the production of [[ATP]].

## Stages
1. [[Glycolysis]] (in cytoplasm)
2. [[Pyruvate Oxidation]] & [[Krebs Cycle]] (in [[Mitochondria]] matrix)
3. [[Oxidative Phosphorylation]] (ETC and [[Chemiosmosis]] across inner mitochondrial membrane)

Overall equation: C6H12O6 + 6O2 -> 6CO2 + 6H2O + Energy (ATP + heat). Produces roughly 36-38 ATP per glucose.
