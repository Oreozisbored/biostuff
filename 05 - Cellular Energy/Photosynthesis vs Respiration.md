# Photosynthesis vs Respiration

## Overview
[[Photosynthesis]] and [[Cellular Respiration]] are complementary processes.

- **Photosynthesis**: Stores energy in organic molecules. Endergonic. Removes CO2, adds O2.
- **Respiration**: Releases energy from organic molecules. Exergonic. Removes O2, adds CO2.

The products of one are the reactants of the other, forming a continuous cycle of matter and flow of energy in ecosystems.
