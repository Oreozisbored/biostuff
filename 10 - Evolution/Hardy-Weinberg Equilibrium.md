# Hardy-Weinberg Equilibrium

## Overview
The state of a population in which frequencies of alleles and genotypes remain constant from generation to generation.

Indicates that the population is NOT evolving. Requires 5 conditions: no mutations, random mating, no natural selection, very large population size, no gene flow. Equations: p + q = 1, and p^2 + 2pq + q^2 = 1.
