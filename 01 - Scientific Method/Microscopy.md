# Microscopy

## Overview
Microscopy is the use of microscopes to view objects too small to be seen with the naked eye.

- **Light Microscopes**: Use light and lenses. Good for living cells.
- **Electron Microscopes**: Use electron beams. High magnification and resolution, used for dead specimens to see organelles.

An essential tool in [[Technology in Biology]].
