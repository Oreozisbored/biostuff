# The Scientific Method

## Overview
The [[Scientific Method]] is a systematic process used to explore observations and answer questions. It is the core process of science.

## Steps
1. Make an [[Observation]]
2. Form a [[Hypothesis]]
3. Design a [[Controlled Experiment]]
4. Collect [[Data]] (Quantitative vs Qualitative)
5. Analyze results and draw a conclusion
6. Submit for [[Peer Review]]

## Key Concepts
It relies on both [[Inductive Reasoning]] and [[Deductive Reasoning]].
