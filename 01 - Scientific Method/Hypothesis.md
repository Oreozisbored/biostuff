# Hypothesis

## Overview
A hypothesis is a proposed, testable, and falsifiable explanation for a narrow set of observations. 

## Key Characteristics
- Must be **testable** through a [[Controlled Experiment]].
- Must be **falsifiable** (capable of being proven false).
- Used in the [[Scientific Method]] alongside [[Deductive Reasoning]].
