# Theory vs Hypothesis

## Overview
In the [[Scientific Method]], there is a distinct difference between a hypothesis and a theory.

- **[[Hypothesis]]**: A testable prediction for a specific observation.
- **Theory**: A broad explanation supported by vast, diverse, and consistent scientific evidence (e.g., Theory of [[Evolution]]).

In everyday language, "theory" often means a guess, which leads to confusion with the scientific definition.
