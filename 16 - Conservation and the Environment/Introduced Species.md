# Introduced Species

## Overview
A species moved by humans, either intentionally or accidentally, from its native location to a new geographic region.

Also called invasive species. They often spread rapidly and outcompete native species because they lack their natural predators/diseases (e.g., Kudzu vine, Zebra mussels). See [[Threats to Biodiversity]].
