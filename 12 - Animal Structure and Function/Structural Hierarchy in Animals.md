# Structural Hierarchy in Animals

## Overview
Animals are multicellular organisms with their bodies organized in a hierarchy of increasing complexity:
Cells → Tissues → Organs → [[Organ Systems Overview|Organ Systems]] → Organism.

Different types of tissues include [[Epithelial Tissue]], [[Connective Tissue]], [[Muscle Tissue]], and [[Nervous Tissue]].
