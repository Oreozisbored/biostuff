# Interphase

## Overview
The period in the [[Cell Cycle]] when the cell is not dividing. It accounts for about 90% of the cell cycle.

During interphase, cellular metabolic activity is high, chromosomes and organelles are duplicated, and cell size may increase.
Consists of:
- [[G1 Phase]]
- [[S Phase]]
- [[G2 Phase]]
