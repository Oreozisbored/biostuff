# Mitosis

## Overview
A process of nuclear division in eukaryotic cells conventionally divided into four stages: [[Prophase]], [[Metaphase]], [[Anaphase]], and [[Telophase]].

Mitosis conserves chromosome number by allocating replicated chromosomes equally to each of the daughter nuclei. Produces two identical diploid [[Somatic Cells]]. See [[Mitosis vs Meiosis]].
