# Gametogenesis

## Overview
The process by which [[Gametes]] are produced via [[Meiosis]].

- **Spermatogenesis**: Produces 4 equal, functional sperm cells.
- **Oogenesis**: Produces 1 large functional egg cell and 3 tiny polar bodies (which degrade) due to unequal [[Cytokinesis]].
