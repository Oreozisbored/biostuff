# Cell Cycle

## Overview
An ordered sequence of events in the life of a cell, from its origin in the division of a parent cell until its own division into two.

It consists of [[Interphase]] (including G1, S, and G2) and M phase ([[Mitosis]] and [[Cytokinesis]]). It is heavily regulated by [[Cell Cycle Checkpoints]].
