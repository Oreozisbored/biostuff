# Mitosis vs Meiosis

## Overview
**Mitosis**:
- 1 division, 2 cells produced.
- Produces identical diploid [[Somatic Cells]].
- Purpose: Growth, repair, replacement.

**Meiosis**:
- 2 divisions, 4 cells produced.
- Produces unique haploid [[Gametes]].
- Purpose: Sexual reproduction, genetic variation.
