# Survivorship Curves

## Overview
A plot of the number of members of a cohort that are still alive at each age; one way to represent age-specific mortality.

- **Type I**: Low death rates early/mid-life, drops steeply in old age (Humans).
- **Type II**: Constant death rate over lifespan (Birds).
- **Type III**: High death rates for young, evens out for survivors (Fish, oysters).
