# 10 Percent Rule

## Overview
The rule of thumb that only about 10% of the energy stored in organic matter at one trophic level is converted to organic matter at the next trophic level.

The remaining 90% is lost, largely as heat from cellular respiration, or in feces. This is why food chains rarely extend beyond 4 or 5 levels. See [[Energy Pyramid]].
