# Nitrogen Fixation

## Overview
The conversion of atmospheric nitrogen (N2) to ammonia (NH3) or ammonium (NH4+).

Biological nitrogen fixation is carried out by certain prokaryotes, some of which have mutualistic relationships with plants (e.g., Rhizobium in root nodules of legumes). Crucial part of the [[Nitrogen Cycle]].
