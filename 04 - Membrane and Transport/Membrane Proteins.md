# Membrane Proteins

## Overview
Proteins embedded in or attached to the [[Cell Membrane]] that perform a variety of functions.

- **Integral Proteins**: Penetrate the hydrophobic core.
- **Peripheral Proteins**: Bound to the surface.
- **Functions**: Transport (see [[Passive Transport]], [[Active Transport]]), enzymatic activity, signal transduction, cell-cell recognition, intercellular joining, and attachment to the [[Cytoskeleton]].
