# Sodium-Potassium Pump

## Overview
A crucial transport protein in the [[Cell Membrane]] of animal cells that actively transports sodium out of the cell and potassium into the cell against their gradients.

It uses [[ATP]] (an example of [[Active Transport]]) and helps maintain resting membrane potential in [[Neurons]]. Moves 3 Na+ OUT and 2 K+ IN.
