# Plant vs Animal Cells

## Overview
Both are [[Eukaryotic Cells]], but have key differences:

**Plant Cells Unique Features:**
- [[Cell Wall]] (cellulose)
- [[Chloroplasts]] (for photosynthesis)
- Large central [[Vacuoles]]

**Animal Cells Unique Features:**
- [[Lysosomes]]
- [[Centrioles]] / centrosomes
- Many small vacuoles (if present)
