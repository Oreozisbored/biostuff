# Lysosomes

## Overview
A membrane-enclosed sac of hydrolytic [[Enzymes]] found in the cytoplasm of animal cells and some protists.

Used to digest (break down via [[Hydrolysis]]) macromolecules, recycle cell materials (autophagy), and destroy pathogens via [[Phagocytosis]].
