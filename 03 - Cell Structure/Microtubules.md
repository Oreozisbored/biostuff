# Microtubules

## Overview
Hollow rods made of tubulin [[Proteins]]. The thickest fiber of the [[Cytoskeleton]].

They shape and support the cell, serve as tracks for organelle movement via motor proteins, and form the spindle fibers during [[Mitosis]]. Also form cilia and flagella (9+2 arrangement).
