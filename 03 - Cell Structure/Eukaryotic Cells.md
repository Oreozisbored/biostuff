# Eukaryotic Cells

## Overview
Cells that contain a true membrane-bound [[Nucleus]] and various other organelles (like [[Mitochondria]], [[Endoplasmic Reticulum]], etc.).

They are generally larger and more complex than [[Prokaryotic Cells]]. Comprise the domain Eukarya (plants, animals, fungi, protists).
