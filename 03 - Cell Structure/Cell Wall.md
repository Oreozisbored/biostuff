# Cell Wall

## Overview
A protective layer external to the [[Cell Membrane]] in plant cells, bacteria, fungi, and some protists.

- **Plants**: Made of cellulose ([[Carbohydrates]]).
- **Fungi**: Made of chitin.
- **Bacteria**: Made of peptidoglycan.

Provides shape, support, and prevents excessive water uptake (see [[Tonicity]]).
