# Vacuoles

## Overview
Large [[Vesicles]] derived from the ER and [[Golgi Apparatus]].

- **Central Vacuole**: Large, in mature plant cells, stores water, ions, and maintains turgor pressure (see [[Tonicity]]).
- **Contractile Vacuole**: Pumps excess water out of some freshwater protists.
- **Food Vacuoles**: Formed by [[Phagocytosis]].
