# Cell Theory

## Overview
A fundamental biological theory.

## Three Tenets
1. All living things are composed of cells.
2. Cells are the basic units of structure and function in living things.
3. New cells are produced from existing cells (via [[Mitosis]] or [[Meiosis]]).

Developed historically by scientists like Hooke, Leeuwenhoek, Schleiden, Schwann, and Virchow.
