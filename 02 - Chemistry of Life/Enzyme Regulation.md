# Enzyme Regulation

## Overview
Cells control when and where its various [[Enzymes]] are active.

- **Competitive Inhibition**: Inhibitor binds to the active site.
- **Noncompetitive Inhibition**: Inhibitor binds elsewhere (allosteric site), changing the active site's shape.
- Factors like temperature and [[pH Scale]] also regulate activity.
