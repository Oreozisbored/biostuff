# Cohesion and Adhesion

## Overview
- **Cohesion**: The sticking together of particles of the same substance (water molecules hydrogen bonding to each other). Causes surface tension.
- **Adhesion**: The sticking together of particles of different substances (water to plant cell walls).

Crucial for capillary action and transport in plants. See [[Water Properties]].
