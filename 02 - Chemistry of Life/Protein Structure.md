# Protein Structure

## Overview
Protein function depends heavily on its structure, which has 4 levels:

1. **Primary**: Sequence of [[Amino Acids]].
2. **Secondary**: Alpha helices and beta pleated sheets due to [[Hydrogen Bonds]] in the backbone.
3. **Tertiary**: Overall 3D shape due to R-group interactions.
4. **Quaternary**: Multiple polypeptide chains coming together.

Loss of this structure is called [[Denaturation]].
