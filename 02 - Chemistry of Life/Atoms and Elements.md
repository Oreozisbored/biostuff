# Atoms and Elements

## Overview
An element is a substance that cannot be broken down to other substances. An atom is the smallest unit of matter that still retains the properties of an element.

## Structure
- Protons (positive) and Neutrons (neutral) in the nucleus.
- Electrons (negative) orbiting in shells.
Atomic number = protons. Mass number = protons + neutrons.
