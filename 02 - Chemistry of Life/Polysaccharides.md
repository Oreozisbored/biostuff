# Polysaccharides

## Overview
Polymers of many [[Monosaccharides]] linked together by [[Dehydration Synthesis]].

- **Storage**: Starch (plants), Glycogen (animals).
- **Structural**: Cellulose (plant cell walls), Chitin (fungi cell walls, arthropod exoskeletons).
