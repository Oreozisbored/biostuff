# pH Scale

## Overview
A logarithmic scale used to specify the acidity or basicity of an aqueous solution. It measures the concentration of H+ ions.

- 0-6: Acidic (more H+)
- 7: Neutral
- 8-14: Basic (less H+, more OH-)

Changes are moderated by [[Buffers]]. See also [[Acids and Bases]].
