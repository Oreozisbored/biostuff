# Functional Groups

## Overview
Specific configurations of atoms commonly attached to the carbon skeletons of [[Organic Molecules]] and involved in chemical reactions.

Examples: Hydroxyl (-OH), Carbonyl (C=O), Carboxyl (-COOH), Amino (-NH2), Phosphate (-OPO3^2-). They determine the chemical properties of molecules like [[Amino Acids]] and [[Fatty Acids]].
